Brand Identity

Valon Mortgage is the warm, knowledgeable partner beside every homeowner — not the slick salesperson in front of them.

Mission

We're on a mission to empower every homeowner, creating a world where homeownership comes with ease, security, and financial know-how.

Tagline

"There's no place like home ownership"

Audience

Current and prospective homeowners — first-time buyers, refinancers, homeowners managing existing mortgages.

Positioning

Modern fintech alternative to legacy servicers. Technology-forward, human-centered. Transparent about rates, fees, and process.

Tone of Voice

Write like a knowledgeable person, not a compliance document. Every word earns its place.

Core Attributes

Warm, not cheerful

We care about homeowners genuinely. Avoid hollow enthusiasm. Be sincere.

Plain, not dumbed down

Mortgage is complex — meet people where they are. Use domain language when it helps; always contextualize it.

Confident, not pushy

State what we offer and let it stand. No pressure tactics, no manufactured urgency.

Human, not robotic

Contractions are fine. Short sentences are fine. Write like a helpful colleague, not a bot.

Voice in Practice

Context Use this Not this
Hero headline"Make the most of your home""The #1 mortgage platform!"
Product copy"Apply in 10–15 minutes""Seamless, frictionless experience"
Error state"Something went wrong. Try again or call us.""Oops! That's on us 😬"
Support"Real people, available when you need them""24/7 omnichannel support"
CTA"See my options""Get started today!"
Rate claim"Competitive rates — see what you qualify for""Industry's lowest rates, guaranteed!"

Words

Use

foundation partner clarity straightforward protection equity security guidance your home your loan transparency

Avoid

seamless frictionless game-changing disrupting best-in-class synergies cutting-edge solutions guaranteed approval empower

Headlines

  • 3–8 words. Declarative. Lead with what the homeowner gains.
  • State a benefit, not a feature.
  • Use sentence case. Never ALL CAPS for emphasis.
  • Active voice. Subject → verb → object.

CTAs

Primary actions should be direct and first-person where possible.

Avoid vague CTAs: Learn more, Click here, Submit. Avoid manufactured urgency: Act now!, Limited time!

Warm, documentary style. Real moments in real homes — never staged stock.

Warm, candid home moments. Real families in real spaces.

Diverse homeowners across life stages — families, first-time buyers, seniors.

Documentary/cinematic. Warm tones, natural light, unposed subjects.
Avoid
Generic "finance" imagery — charts, documents, and data with no human or home context.

10

Page-Specific Guidelines

How the brand standards apply to each major section of the site.

Home
  • Hero leads with the homeowner's goal, not Valon's capabilities
  • Primary CTAs: "See my options" or "Get a mortgage"
  • Social proof (reviews, NMLS) visible above the fold or immediately below hero
Get a Mortgage / Apply
  • Reinforce speed early: "Apply in 10–15 minutes"
  • Progress indicators on multi-step forms — always show where the user is
  • Error messages must be specific and actionable (not "Invalid input")
Manage Your Mortgage
  • Empathetic framing — users here may be in reactive mode
  • Lead with support availability: phone, email, portal access
  • Surface common actions first: make a payment, view statement, contact support
Insurance
  • Plain language about coverage — avoid policy jargon
  • Keep the directness of "Great coverage. Great price." throughout
  • Calculator must show results without requiring account creation
Home Equity (HELOC / HELOAN / Debt Consolidation)
  • Lead with use cases, not product names ("pay off debt", not "HELOAN")
  • Calculators update in real time — no submit required
  • Disclose rates and terms clearly; never bury APR in footnotes
Blog (Inside Valon · Valon Tech Talk)
  • Bylines required on all posts
  • Educational value over SEO stuffing
  • Tech Talk posts may use more domain-specific language

Required on every page. Non-negotiable in regulated mortgage content.

NMLS ID #1907140 must appear in the footer of all pages. Licensed in all 50 states + DC — licensing page link required in footer.

Rates and APR must be displayed with required disclosures. "Loans subject to approval" or equivalent must accompany any rate or payment example.

Links to Privacy Policy, Terms & Conditions, and Data Sharing Consent required in footer.

Do not use "guarantee" or "guaranteed approval" in any copy — ever.
